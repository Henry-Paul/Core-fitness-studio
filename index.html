Who We Are

Core Fitness Studio is a Trademark and GST registered gym head-quartered in Nallagandla and has been operational for over 5 years. We don't just provide service and maintenance; we deliver a legacy.

The owner, a Certified Nutrition Coach, has been in the fitness industry for over 20 years and possesses a deep, scientific understanding of the human body and transformational mechanics.

Our team at CFS comes with an overall collective experience of over 30 years. Our multiple trainers continue to actively participate and win prestigious competitions at various levels. We are passionate about what we do, and that is what makes us stand tall amongst mortals.
